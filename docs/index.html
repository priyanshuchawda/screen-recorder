Built Around the Native Pipeline

A high-efficiency recording utility built on top of native Windows graphics and media framework APIs.

Hardware-First Encoding

Direct integration with GPU hardware encoders (Intel QuickSync / NVENC / AMF) via Windows Media Foundation.

Mic & System Audio

WASAPI capture engine recording system loopback audio and microphone streams with precise sample sync.

Camera Overlay

Configurable webcam overlay with customizable resolution, frames, mirroring, and low-latency rendering.

Private & Offline

100% offline local utility. No trackers, telemetry, or remote servers. Your videos stay on your drive.

Intuitive Settings

Simple main interface. Toggle camera overlays, microphone capture, and HQ modes with one click.

Crash Resilient

Partial files are flushed incrementally, helping you recover raw recordings if your machine runs out of battery.
